Dance

I started dancing at about the age of 4. During this time, I learned Bollywood dance and a classical form of Indian dance known as Bharatanayam. Once I was 13, I wanted to start branching out and began to learn American dance styles. Now, I am currently learning Ballet, Lyrical/Contemporary, Jazz, Hiphop, and more. I take dance classes at my high school, including a Choreography class. I also have participated in various school dance teams: The Lawrenceville Dance Team (Lyrical/Contemporoary), Tour de Force (Hiphop and Jazz), and Sauve (Spanish dancing). On Tour de Force, I assist in choreographing and leading the team sometimes. Explore the dance page to see videos of dances I have performed or choreographed!
